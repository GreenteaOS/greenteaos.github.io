WELCOME TO GREENTEA OS What is it?

Warm and cozy operating system, free open-source re-implementation of Microsoft® Windows™ NT

Greentea OS is based on ReactOS™ project. ReactOS™ is an Open Source effort to develop a quality operating system that is compatible with applications and drivers written for the Microsoft® Windows™ NT family of operating systems (NT4, 2000, XP, 2003, Vista, Seven).
